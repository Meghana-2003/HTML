about us

Sakleshpur is a little mesmerizing town tucked 220 km away from Bangalore. Located in the foothills of Western Ghats in the region of Malnad, it offers spellbinding views that will take your breath away. Lush green hills of this fascinating town are abundantly covered with cardamom, coffee and pepper plantations that are perfect for a magical surprise. There are many places to visit in Sakleshpur. This quaint hill station offers a delightful experience for naturalists and breathtaking trails for trekking enthusiasts. Some of the most popular places to visit in and around Sakleshpur include a trove of majestic temples, monumental forts, enchanting waterfalls, spectacular mountain hikes, and biodiversity hotspots. If you are going to Bangalore anytime soon, plan a getaway to Sakleshpur as well for an extraordinary experience.
